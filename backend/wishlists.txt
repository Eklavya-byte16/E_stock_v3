WISHLIST MODEL
 * Stores the stocks a user wants to watch, along with a computed
 * growth score that is updated whenever fresh market data comes in.
 *
 * Growth Score (0–100) is a weighted composite of:
 *   40%  momentum   — recent price trend direction
 *   30%  volume     — unusual buying/selling interest
 *   20%  fundamental— P/E, EPS, revenue trend
 *   10%  sentiment  — analyst ratings + news score
